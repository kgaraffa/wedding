The Story Of Us

August
2014

When we met

After getting home from pick up soccer game, Kate's upstairs neighbor, Alex, was enjoying a beer on the roof with friends. Alex invited Kate up their first sundowner they'd enjoy together.

November
2014

Our first 'date'

Alex, a lover of everything extreme, invited Kate to hike up Bolton Valley and spend the night winter camping to get first tracks after a night of snowmaking. Alex soon learned that it was Kate's second time ever camping.

July
2020

The proposal

Alex invited Kate's family out for a day sail. To celebrate the day, we sailed over to Fisherman's Island to shoot their cannon off. As Alex bent down to get the cannon ready, he pulled out the ring and asked Kate to sail around the world with him.

November
2021

Our wedding

We cannot wait to tie the knot back in Key West and show our family and friends a very special place to us.
